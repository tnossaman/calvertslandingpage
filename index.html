Our History

Rooted in
Oklahoma

Calvert's Plant Interiors has been Oklahoma's most trusted name in live plant design since 1976. Our grounds have served as a greenhouse since 1907 — before Oklahoma was even a state.

We are a small, locally owned business with a wide reach. Our designers, maintenance team, and greenhouse staff bring the resources and passion to make any space feel alive.

Nationally recognized — proud members of the National Interiorscape Network, Green Plants for Green Buildings, BOMA, IFMA, and AIA.
